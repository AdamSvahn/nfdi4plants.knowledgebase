The investigation level allows you to record project metadata such as the project title and description, responsible contacts and associated publications.

<Card icon="pen" title="ARCitect">

<Steps>

1. Click on the ARC's name (`AthalianaColdStressSugar`) on top of the file tree
2. A panel opening to the right shows the investigation form
3. Here you can add metadata about your investigation

    :::note[Identifier]
    The Investigation identifier was automatically added based on your ARC's name
    :::

4. Add a **Title**

    - Meaningful project title (with blanks)
    - Avoid administrative metadata

    <ViolaSays>

    The **Title** of my project is _The effect of cold stress on sugar metabolism in *A. thaliana*_.

    </ViolaSays>

4. Add a **Description**

    - Concise project abstract 

    <ViolaSays>

    We investigate the effect of cold stress on sugar metabolism in *A. thaliana*. We grew *A. thaliana* plants under cold and ambient conditions in a two-day time series experiment. Leave protein and sugar pools were assayed via proteomics and metabolomics.

    </ViolaSays>

4. Add a **Contacts**

    - Persons responsible for the project 

    <ViolaSays>

    I add my PI *Prof. Jasmine Beetroot*, our collaboration partner *Oliver Sage* and myself as **Contacts**.

    </ViolaSays>

</Steps>

</Card>
